Arduino RFID Module

Scans RFID tags. Effective at 10cm.

In this project, we are planning on implementing an optional locking mechanism that uses an RFID scanner and servo motor to keep personal vehicles in place while they are being charged. Items containing RFID tags are found everywhere and many student ID's come with these tags built in. They have lots of information in them but the only thing that will be scannable is the tag's Serial Number, which is enough to act as a key for a personal lock.
